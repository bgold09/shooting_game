Project 02 - Target Shooting Game

How to Play

  • The object of the game is to hit all of the targets and miss as few times as possible.
  • Firing Projectiles
    • To fire a projectile, choose an angle and velocity
    • You may use either the text boxes or sliders provided to enter values
    • Then click "FIRE!" to launch the projectile with the given inputs
    • A random bird will be launched each time
  • The Birds
    • Orange birds are the largest of all birds. They are the easiest to hit a target with!
    • Yellow birds will bounce one time after hitting the ground. You might be able to hit a target even if you are short!
    • Red birds will head straight down after reaching their max height. Even if you overshoot you still might hit a target!
    • Blue birds behave normally, but they are the smallest and are the most difficult to hit with
  • Scoring
    • You gain 5 points for each target you hit
    • You lose 1 point for each time you miss
  • Click the reset button to revive the targets and start over with zero points
